# GOODFEED

Instagram is designed to be loud. GOODFEED tones it down.

## What it does

- **Grayscale** — removes color from the entire page, making it far less stimulating to scroll through
- **Blur media** — photos and videos are blurred by default. Click to reveal, and they fade back after 5 seconds
- **No autoplay** — videos stay paused until you choose to watch them

## Why

Constant color, motion, and auto-playing content keep our nervous system in a low-grade state of alert and distraction. GOODFEED strips away the noise so you can browse with intention — and leave when you meant to.

## Install

Load unpacked in `chrome://extensions` with Developer Mode on.
